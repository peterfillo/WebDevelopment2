About Us

Who we are

Fit Nest is a fitness resource website that was developed by 3 people (Samuel Angus, Joshua Chiu, Sealtiel Villegas) with the intention to improve the health of people who have stay-at-home lifestyles. With eyes constantly glued to computer and phone screens, it's easy for many to neglect their physical and mental health.

To prevent exhaustion and loss of motivation from browsing through many different sites, Fit Nest provides condensed and organized information regarding different aspects of health.

Our Vision

To inspire people to live healthy lives despite the limitations and challenges.

Our Mission

To encourage people to improve their health, we provide an all-in-one service to promote the holistic health of our users. The user experience and services we provide are designed to benefit the mind and body.
